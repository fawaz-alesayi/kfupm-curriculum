Help

KFUPM Curriculum System can be used easily. After entring Curriculum system, you can either search for any course directly in the top of the page, or by scrolling down and choose the subject under its college. You will see all courses of the chosen subject, click on your searched course and you will see all related stuffs about the course such as Description, Outcomes ... etc. You can click on (KFUPM Curriculum word at the top lift of the page to go back to the home page. You will see Most three viewed courses in home page, so that you can see most required courses in KFUPM.
